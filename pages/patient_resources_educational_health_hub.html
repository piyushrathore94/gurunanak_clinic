Treatment Preparation & Aftercare

Maximize your treatment outcomes with proper preparation and aftercare

Before Treatment

  • Avoid sun exposure 48 hours before laser treatments
  • Stop retinoids 1 week before chemical peels
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Inform about medications and allergies
  • Stay hydrated and get adequate sleep

During Treatment

  • Communicate any discomfort immediately
  • Follow all safety instructions carefully
  • Remain still during laser procedures
  • Ask questions about the procedure
  • Take notes on aftercare instructions

After Treatment

  • Apply prescribed creams as directed
  • Avoid direct sunlight for 48-72 hours
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free products
  • Schedule follow-up appointments
  • Contact clinic if unusual reactions occur

Common Skin Conditions Guide

Comprehensive information about skin conditions common in the Indian population

Acne & Pimples

Common inflammatory skin condition affecting teenagers and adults, especially in humid climates.

Symptoms: Blackheads, whiteheads, inflamed bumps
Causes: Hormones, genetics, pollution, diet
Treatment: Topical retinoids, antibiotics, laser therapy

Eczema

Chronic inflammatory condition causing dry, itchy, and inflamed skin patches.

Symptoms: Dry, itchy, red patches
Triggers: Allergens, stress, weather changes
Treatment: Moisturizers, topical steroids, lifestyle changes

Psoriasis

Autoimmune condition causing rapid skin cell buildup and scaly patches.

Symptoms: Thick, scaly, silver patches
Triggers: Stress, infections, medications
Treatment: Topical treatments, phototherapy, biologics

Vitiligo

Condition causing loss of skin pigmentation, creating white patches on the skin.

Symptoms: White patches, hair color changes
Causes: Autoimmune, genetic factors
Treatment: Topical steroids, phototherapy, surgery

Melasma

Hormonal pigmentation disorder causing dark patches, common in women.

Symptoms: Dark patches on face
Triggers: Pregnancy, sun exposure, hormones
Treatment: Chemical peels, laser therapy, topical lighteners

Fungal Infections

Common in humid climates, affecting various parts of the body including feet and groin.

Symptoms: Itching, scaling, redness
Causes: Moisture, poor hygiene, tight clothing
Treatment: Antifungal creams, oral medications, hygiene
