Our Story & Journey

From a vision to serve the Kanhaiya Nagar community to becoming Delhi's trusted dermatology destination

Our Founding Vision

"In 2018, we recognized that Kanhaiya Nagar and surrounding areas lacked accessible, expert dermatological care. Our mission was simple: bring world-class skin treatment to our community with the warmth and personal attention that every patient deserves."

Dr. Rajesh Kumar founded Guru Nanak Skin Clinic with a commitment to combining medical excellence with compassionate care. Having trained at premier institutions like AIIMS and PGI Chandigarh, he wanted to make advanced dermatological treatments accessible to families in Delhi's growing suburban communities.

What started as a small clinic with a big vision has grown into a comprehensive dermatology center, serving over 8,000 patients and maintaining a 98% satisfaction rate. Our success is built on trust, transparency, and genuine care for each patient's skin health journey.

Our Core Philosophy

  • Patient-centered care with personalized treatment plans
  • Evidence-based treatments with proven results
  • Transparent communication in Hindi and English
  • Continuous education and latest treatment innovations

Patient Safety & Hygiene Standards

Our Safety Commitment

Advanced Sterilization

All instruments undergo autoclave sterilization at 134°C for 18 minutes, exceeding WHO standards. Single-use disposables for all invasive procedures.

Clean Air Systems

HEPA filtration systems in all treatment rooms with positive air pressure maintenance. Regular air quality monitoring and UV sanitization.

Personal Protective Equipment

All staff use medical-grade PPE including N95 masks, face shields, and sterile gloves. Fresh protective equipment for every patient interaction.

Surface Disinfection

Hospital-grade disinfectants used on all surfaces between patients. UV-C sanitization of treatment rooms during non-operational hours.
